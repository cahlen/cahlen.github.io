:: About Me ::

Hello! I'm Cahlen Humphreys – a passionate technologist based in Irvine, CA. As the Managing Principal at Enfuse.io, I specialize in AI Software Engineering, Edge AI, LLMs, Computer Vision, Distributed Systems, and Real-Time Analytics.

I’m a big fan of artificial intelligence, mathematics, and computer science, with a keen interest in neural networks—pushing boundaries using techniques like continued fraction neural networks to enhance interpretability and explainability in AI.

Beyond the technical realm, I enjoy motorcycles, heavy metal music (I even participated in a music video for Slaughter to Prevail), and engaging with the tech community through open source contributions and speaking engagements.
